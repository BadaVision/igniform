Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data Controller

The controller of your personal data is STAMP POL Mirosław Stachowiak, with its registered office at ul. Zagrodowa 47, 63-200 Jarocin, Poland, VAT ID: PL6171359573. For matters related to the processing and protection of personal data, you can contact us at the following e-mail address: info@igniform.eu.

2. Scope, Purpose, and Legal Basis of Data Processing

We process your personal data (name, surname, e-mail address, company name, and optionally phone number) provided voluntarily via the contact form available on the website.

This data is processed solely for the purpose of:

  • Responding to the submitted inquiry and conducting business correspondence.
  • Presenting a commercial offer at the explicit request of the User.

The legal basis for data processing is taking steps at the request of the data subject prior to entering into a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) of the GDPR) and our legitimate interest in handling inquiries (Art. 6(1)(f) of the GDPR).

3. Data Recipients

We treat your personal data with the utmost confidentiality. We do not sell or share it for marketing purposes with third parties. Recipients of the data may only be trusted entities processing data on our behalf to maintain the technical infrastructure (e.g., hosting and e-mail service providers), based on relevant data processing agreements.

4. Data Retention Period

Your personal data will be stored for the period necessary to handle the inquiry and conduct correspondence, and may then be archived for the period required to defend against potential legal claims (in accordance with applicable law).

5. User Rights

In accordance with GDPR regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access the content of your data and receive a copy thereof.
  • Rectify (correct) your data.
  • Request the erasure of data (the right to be forgotten), provided there are no overriding legal grounds for further processing.
  • Restrict data processing.
  • Object to the processing.
  • Data portability.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at: info@igniform.eu. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority – the President of the Personal Data Protection Office (PUODO).

6. Cookies and Tracking

We respect your privacy. Our website is purely informative and does not use any tracking, analytical mechanisms (such as Google Analytics), or marketing cookies.
